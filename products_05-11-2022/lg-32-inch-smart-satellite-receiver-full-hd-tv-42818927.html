Buying an Updated TV is what You Need

Here are a few things to keep in mind when buying your new TV; the size of the TV screen: How big (or small) of a screen should you get for your room? Think about where you want to put your TV and what your budget is. LCD TV Size: 19 inches to 65 inches. Pros: Consumes a lot less power than plasma; models with matte screens work well in bright rooms. LED TV Size: 32 inches and larger. Pros: Uses slightly less power than LCD; thin panels; some models have improved picture quality.Best for big spenders who want thin panels or videophiles who understand the differences between LED technologies and don't want plasma. Plasma TV Size: 42 inches and larger. Manufacturers: Panasonic, Samsung, LG. Pros: Black-level performance in dark rooms; uniformity and viewing angle. Best for: General use in all but the brightest rooms.
