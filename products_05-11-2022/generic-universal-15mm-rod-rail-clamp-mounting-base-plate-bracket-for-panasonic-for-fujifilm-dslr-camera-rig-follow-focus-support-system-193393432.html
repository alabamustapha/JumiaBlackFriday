Type A Upgraded Version:

Features:

New design, aircr aft carrier shape, extreme hollow.

With 3/8"-16 and 1/4" -20 screws, it can be easily and quickly connected to photographic equipment with 1/4" or 3/8" screw holes;

The product is designed with rich expansion holes, with 1/4 3/8 ARRI positioning holes and many 1/4 holes respectively. There is no preure to install expansion acceories;

The bottom plate is designed with 4x silicone protective rubber pads, and the equipment is installed on it to prevent scratches and prevent the installation screw from loosening due to vibration;

There are 2x 1/4" screw through holes with a hole spacing of 18mm at the bottom, which can be installed on shoulder pads and other acceories;

The bottom is designed with a 38mm tripod groove, which can be quickly installed on the quick clamping base, just like a tripod;

The bottom plate is designed with a guide rod hole with a diameter of 15mm, which can be installed with a scorer, a shade box, a lens holder, and other acceories
