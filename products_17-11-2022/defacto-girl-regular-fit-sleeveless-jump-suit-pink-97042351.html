Teen Girls Playsuit & jumpsuits On Jumia

Usually what we see in fashion shows cannot be replicated in real life simply because fashion show costumes are dramatized. We need to take inspiration from those clothes and make a less exaggerated version of them. Jumpsuits are one such item which is overly done on the runway with accessories. When wearing a jumpsuit out in the city you effortlessly throw on a crossover bag and you are good to go. Ladies jumpsuits price may vary from style to style but you can buy cheap online jumpsuits as well.

Jumpsuits for the Fashionista in you

Women playsuits in Nigeria are the best way to transition into summer as they have a very effortless appeal to them. Jumpsuits with a wider leg can help you give an hourglass figure where the waist can be cinched in with a belt and you sort of have high rise pants on the bottom. Jumpsuits with cropped pants are just perfect for someone who wants to show off their legs too. These jumpsuits have elastic at the end of the pants. They blouse over your calves giving a sassy outfit perfect for the beach. The waist elastic is also covered with a top blouse and a drawstring. These jumpsuits have a very hip-hop grove to them. With these jumpsuits you can wear flats for the day, something bright and strappy will stand out and for the night heels go perfectly.

The Right Jumpsuit for You

For girls who are slightly short height should opt for the classic flared pant jumpsuit. The extra length of the pants can help you cover tall wedges making them look like they are never-ending. The deep v neckline may also give an illusion of a taller neck. Girls who want to show off their collar bones or if they are wearing a statement necklace, they should opt for a jumpsuit with a tube top. Online shopping in Nigeria for these styles in jumpsuits can be a little difficult. Jumia, however, offers the widest range in apparel for all body types.

Ladies Playsuits Styles

The solid colored jumpsuit alone is enough to make a bold statement but for the evening you can dress it up with gold or silver accessories. Other than the usual jewelry, a belt always looks edgy with a jumpsuit. There are many options in belts to break the outfit and the monotony of a jumpsuit. You can wear the classic chain belt which again comes in many types of links. If you want to tone down the jumpsuit from all its fabulousness then layer a well-fit blazer with the jumpsuit. But remember not to pick the same shades. A black blazer looks great with an emerald green jumpsuit; pink cropped blazer with a black jumpsuit will look feminine yet bold.
