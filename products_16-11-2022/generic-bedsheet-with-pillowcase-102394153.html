Fitted, Elastic Sheets or Plain Sheets?

Choosing between the plain bed sheets and fitted sheets with elastic running along depends on the environment at your home. If you have kids at home, you cannot afford to have a plain cloth sheet; in this case you require a fitted sheet to make sure that it fits snug as a bug over your mattress and it does not move around when your kids dance on the bed or when you toss and turn during the night. How Do Bed Sheets feel?

Before you purchase a sheet, you need to figure out the kind of feel you are expecting from it. Some people love super soft sheets whereas others prefer a clean crisp feel. For some people, wrinkles on the sheet drive them mad. So if you are seeking soft breeze bed sheets in Nigeria you need opting for 100% cotton bed sheets. On the other hand, if you want a wrinkle-free sheet, you should go for cotton and polyester blended sheets. 100% silk bed sheets in Nigeria are also ideal for those seeking soft and wrinkle-free bed sheet sets.

Advantages of particular pillow and bed sheet fabrics

There are plentiful materials that make up your bed sheets and pillows. Silk is considered as one of the royal entities because of its glow and smoothness. Silk pillows are finest to maintain the freshness of the human face. Silk gives a charming shine to the skin surface and also retains the moisture. It also reduces the appearance of wrinkles and keeps the hair healthy. Cotton bed sheets are good for those people who are prone to allergies. Cotton fabric is very soft and smoothly handles the sensitivity of the human skin. Cotton adopts itself in all sort of weathers, means it will protect the skin in both summer and winter season. Linen sheets are moisture absorbing and more ventilating than other fabrics. The bacteria resistant characteristic help to keep the infections and diseases away. The breathable feature maintains the temperature by providing a cool effect in summer and warmth in winter.
