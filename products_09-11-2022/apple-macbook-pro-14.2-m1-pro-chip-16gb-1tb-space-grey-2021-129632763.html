Choosing the Right MacBook

There are a number of features that make the MacBook stand out from other laptop brands. Since the introduction of the MacBook family, Apple have gone to manufacture different types of MacBook with each have a unique feature and spec over the other and of course, they are all expensive but worth having.


MacBook Air: The MacBook Air is a lightweight notebook manufactured by Apple. They are available in two different screen sizes which are 11-inch and 13-inch respectively. It comes with a 1.6GHz dual-core processor which also is either Core i5 or i7 depending on which you want. The RAM size is 4GB and comes with a solid state drive as the internal storage capacity (128GB and 256GB).


MacBook Pro: The MacBook Pro can be differentiated by Retina display. One with the Retina display and the other without it. It is available in three different screen sizes which are 13-inch which is a general purpose Mac with small display screen and Thunderbolt offers a quicker and faster video and data processing which is ideal for students and professionals; 15-inch which comes with an SDCX card slot and an anti-glare display feature which is ideal for gamers and graphics professionals; 17-inch which is similar to the 15-inch but with an Express card and it's ideal for gamers and media professionals. The Mac comes with three different solid state drive options which are 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB and hard disk drive of 320GB, 500GB, and 750GB, these two internal storage devices are presently simultaneously.
