Men'sCasual Watches on Jumia

It is not necessary to buy men’s designer watches only to look classy. There are some casual watches that are superb in terms of look craftsmanship materials and technology. The watch shop at Jumia has a solid collection of well built, day to day watches for men, watches for women and kid’s watches. There are a few Men’s Watch brands that come at Affordable prices such as Casio, Seiko, and Fossil. Whether you are looking for a stainless steel watch, LED watch or watch with a rubber strap, you will find a multitude of options in different brands.

Seiko 5 Automatic – A Casual Watch on Budget

The Seiko 5 Automatic watch for men is really hard to pass up in terms of its overall value. This watch has a nice 21 jewel automatic movement, unlike the quarts. The 37 mm dial is made from a hard glass mineral crystal and stainless steel frame. The back dial has a nice exhibition of all its mechanics. The hour and minute hands of the Seiko watch are luminous and the stay glowing for about twenty minutes. It also has a day and date bar on the right-hand side. The Seiko 5 has a nice wide 18 mm Candice strap which is not only extremely comfortable to wear but also low maintenance. The watch is water resistant to about 30 meters. The overall look of the watch is masculine, sport and adventurous. Even though this is a casual watch but it gives a very luxurious feel. It looks extremely well made and high quality for a watch that is under the USD 200 mark.

The Citizen Men’s Watch Range at Jumia

The Citizen Men’s AT8020-54L is a World Chronograph A-T Watch. The beautiful Eco-Drive Blue Angels watch is fuelled by light. It also has a radio-controlled perpetual calendar chronograph world timer with atomic timekeeping in different time zones. The Citizen BL8000-54L is another Eco-Drive watch. This Calibre 8700 sports watch is powered by the sun. It absorbs the sunlight through the crystal dial. It is loaded with features such as calendar, alarm and dual time function. Buy this watch if you want to give a rugged yet classy look. The Citizen Men’s BL5250-02L is a Titanium watch B000EQS0WK with rich leather straps. It chargers in natural indoor and outdoor lighting, perpetual calendar, sub dial, unidirectional rotating bezel, and tachymeter inner chapter ring.

Features to Look at When Buying a Men’s Watch

Men usually prefer watches with solid construct and masculine detailing. The watch should also be comfortable to wear and not too heavy on the wrist. Light, functional and nice looking watches for men are a hard find. Titanium watches are usually lighter and stronger. Sporty watches and aviator inspired watches are hot favorites amongst young adults. Slightly older people are more comfortable with the classic styles. A classic watch with leather straps, double gold bars, simple design that is easy to see is for the man who has seen it all.
