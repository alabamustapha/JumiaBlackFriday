High Functioning HDMI Cables at Jumia Nigeria

HDMI cables in Nigeria are a necessity for everyone. You need a high functioning and good quality ones that can work well with your devices and give you the viewing pleasure that you want. Search using multiple options at Jumia.com.ng to find one that fulfills all your requirements.

What is an HDMI Cable?

High Definition Multimedia Interface (HDMI) cables are used to carry digital audio and video. This input is used to connect HD equipment such as PVR, Blu-Ray Players and HD boxes to your television. Every television can take a certain number of HDMI TV cable connections. Large screen TVs take up to four maybe even five cables while on the other hand, small screen TVs take up to three cables. More ports can be added to the TV if a switching box or an HDMI converter is attached.

HDMI with Ethernet Cables

HDMI with Ethernet cables give the facility of allowing an Ethernet-connected device to share connections with different components over the HDMI cable. These cables are also backward compatible and can work on devices that are non-Ethernet as well.

Types of HDMI Cables

There are four types of HDMI cables to consider. These are:

High Speed with Ethernet

High Speed without Ethernet

Standard Speed with Ethernet:

Standard Speed without Ethernet

It is recommended to always go with the high-speed one because it comes with the capability to support the current technologies. The standard cables are known to transmit 1080i or 720p video quality. The high-speed ones can display technologies such as 3D and 4K.

Meaning of different versions of HDMI Cables

The components of the HDMI versions are marked with numbers such as (HDMI 1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.2a, 1.3, 1.4, 2.0, or 2.0a). The implementation of these is optional and it varies from manufacturer to manufacturer. It is advised to look at the specific capabilities to the components instead of the version if you want to go for the perfect fit. All of the HDMI versions are known to be compatible with previous versions so they have a widespread use.

HDMI Signal Capability

One consideration to have is the signal capability of the HDMI. This depends from manufacturer to manufacturer. The benefit of HDMI cables is that it does not provide a distance limitation. The factors that define this are the construction and the design of the HDMI cable, the electronic device that is being installed along with the resolution of the video that you want to send. When the cable is long, the quality matters more so that the signal quality is not compromised. If you want to have a signal boosting quality, you need to go with one that is longer than 30 feet.

HDMI ARC

The HDMI Audio Return Channel is a great way that connects a home cinema along with an AV receiver or sound bar as well. It allows you to use a single HDMI lead to send audios from your TV to the sound receivers.
